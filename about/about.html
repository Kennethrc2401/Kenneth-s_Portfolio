Facts - Timeline

  • I took a class in 7th Grade called "Computer Apps" which was about learing about computers, typing without looking at the key board, using Microsoft Excel, etc. I really enjoyed the class. A few weeks into the class, I ended up ordering a book from Amazon, called, "Upgrading and Repairing PCs" by Scott Mueller which was about computers and learned how to build computers. I built a few of them; one of them include the gaming computer that I still use today. All of them worked successfully. I continued learning new things over the years.
  • In 8th Grade, I got into gaming(playing video games) and eventually got into streaming games on Twitch.tv and into uploading videos onto my own YouTube channel.
  • In October 2017, during my Junior year(11th Grade) of high school, I recieved an email from a non-profit organization called, Codecademy, about a 10-week, self-paced, online course called "Build Websites from Scratch" which had and still has a price of $199 which includes the credentials that you recieve upon completion of the course and it was about learning how to build websites from the ground-and-up. I ended up accepting the offer and taking two other courses. One of them was called, "Build Website UIs" and the other was called, "Build Front-End Web Apps". The first one was about making websites more usable and accessibale to everyone, especially, to people who use screen-readers and the second one was about using a programming language called JavaScript to add functionality to websites as well as using it to build websites. I learned so much from these programs. I still build websites today. These coding skills may help you a lot is school if you have "Web Design" as a class or if you're job/career has to do with coding. One thing that was really helpful within these courses was the fact that there was a chatroom on a programm called, "Slack" that was full of people who were there to help you and everyone else who needed help. I would definitely recommend taking one of these Codecademy: Pro Intensives.
